How SkinSpectra Works
A three-layer AI pipeline designed for clinical-grade skincare intelligence.
Layer 1 — NLP Mapping

Uses sentence-transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) to map raw ingredient names to INCI standard nomenclature. Works even with brand names, abbreviations, and common misspellings.

Layer 2 — ML Scoring

XGBoost + rule engine for individual product scoring. LightGBM + rule engine for two-product layering compatibility. Trained on thousands of dermatologist-reviewed formulations.

Layer 3 — LLM Report

Gemini 2.5 Flash synthesises the ML outputs into a personalised, human-readable report with usage tips, routine integration advice, and safety warnings specific to your skin profile.

OCR Extraction

Tesseract 5 LSTM engine with adaptive binarisation, auto-deskew, and INCI-aware post-processing extracts ingredient lists directly from product label photos.
